About School

St. Britto Public School, Malighat, Muzaffarpur did its humble beginning in April 2004 with 35 students in a rented building and part of DAV Public school, Khabra, Darbhanga road. It was shifted to its own building in September 2005. It is an important link of the chain of over 700 such Institutions with a glorious history. It lays emphasis on the alround development of the learners instilling moral values and vedic culture among its learners. Presently it has over 3000 students with start of class - XII from 2014. It has a magnificient building with good laboratories , library cum reading room and well qualified and dedicated staff.
